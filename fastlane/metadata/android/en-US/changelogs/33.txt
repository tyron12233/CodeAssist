CodeAssist 3.0.2

* Your projects now survive app updates, and projects from older versions are recovered automatically. Older-format Gradle projects open in a read-only compatibility view (#1003, #1024, #1041, #1042).
* Dependencies resolve more reliably: an incomplete library (a transitive that failed to download, such as androidx.activity behind activity-compose) is now healed when the project opens, and download failures are reported instead of silently dropped.
* The Kotlin editor now sees unsaved edits in your other open files, with sharper unresolved-type diagnostics and completion.
* Editor shows real parameter names and Javadoc/KDoc pulled from library sources.
* Desktop gains a file-tree right-click context menu and a shared move/copy browser.
* SDK Manager fixes.
* Fixed: the soft keyboard now reappears after being dismissed (#1039).

Full changelog: https://github.com/tyron12233/CodeAssist/releases/tag/v3.0.2
